## Demo Flow (5 Minutes)

### 1. Bridge Overview (Dashboard)
- **Show the stats grid:** 16 crew, 10 expired certs, 5 expiring within 30 days
- **Show the cert timeline:** Red/amber/blue bars showing the certification health
- **Show the crew breakdown by department:** Deck, Engineering, Interior, Galley, Management
- **Show recent agent activity:** Cerberus and Nereus have already been working

**Captain value:** "This is what you'd see every morning instead of opening spreadsheets."

### 2. Crew Manifest (Crew Page)
- **Show the full crew table:** Names, positions, departments, contract dates
- **Filter by department:** Click deck/engineering buttons
- **Click a crew member to drill down**

**Captain value:** "Every crew member, every detail, one place."

### 3. Crew Detail — The Power Page
- **Show the crew profile:** Certifications list with color-coded expiry (red/amber/green)
- **Show the AI Renewal Plan:** The agent has already analyzed which certs need renewal, grouped them by prerequisites, and recommended training providers with ratings
- **Show the Onboarding Checklist:** 10-point checklist auto-generated when crew joins
- **Show the Visas section**

**Captain value:** "The AI doesn't just track — it plans. It knows STCW deps, it knows which training providers are near the vessel, and it tells you exactly what to do."

### 4. Alert Center
- **Show the unresolved alerts:** Critical certs, warnings, filtered by severity
- **Resolve one alert to show the workflow**
- **Point out:** These were auto-generated by Cerberus scanning overnight. No human had to check a spreadsheet.

**Captain value:** "You don't look for problems. The AI finds them and tells you."

### 5. Compliance Center
- **Generate a live MLC 2006 report:** Click the MLC button, watch it generate in real-time
- **Read the findings aloud:** "10 expired certifications found. 16 crew without active SEAs."
- **Read the AI recommendations aloud**
- **Generate an STCW audit report to show a second type**

**Captain value:** "Port state control inspection prep used to take days. Now it takes one click."

### 6. Wrap: The Architecture
- **5 agents running autonomously:** Cerberus (certs), Nereus (rotations), Plutus (payroll), Hermes (reporting), Mentor (recruitment)
- **Not a database you fill in — agents that work while you sleep**
- **Demo vessel is fictional, but every cert date, crew member, and scenario is realistic**

## Questions Captains Will Ask (Be Ready)

**Q: Do I have to type everything in?**
A: Import from CSV or existing crew management software. Most captains already have this data — it's just in spreadsheets.

**Q: Is it offline-capable?**
A: Yes — PWA with offline mode. Syncs when you're back in port.

**Q: What about data privacy?**
A: Encrypted at rest. Your crew data stays on your instance. We never share it.

**Q: How much?**
A: $499-799/month per vessel. Your time is worth $3,000-4,500/month in admin savings alone. Before we talk about avoided detention risk.

**Q: What certs does it track?**
A: STCW, flag state endorsements, ENG1 medical, visas, passports, contract dates — and it understands the dependencies between them.
